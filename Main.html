The 17 Goals Of the United Nations

Goal 1: End poverty in all its forms everywhere


Goal 2: End hunger and achieve food security


Goal 3: Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all


Goal 4: Ensure quality education and lifelong learning opportunities


Goal 5: Achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls


Goal 6: Ensure management of water and sanitation for all


Goal 7: Ensure access to affordable and reliable energy


Goal 8: Promote economic growth, employment work for all


Goal 9: Build infrastructure, promote industrialization and innovation


Goal 10: Reduce inequality within and among countries


Goal 11: Make human settlements inclusive, safe and sustainable


Goal 12: Ensure sustainable consumption and production patterns


Goal 13: Take action to combat climate change and its impacts


Goal 14: Conserve the oceans and marine resources for development


Goal 15: Protect use of ecosystems, forests, and reverse land degradation


Goal 16: Promote peaceful societies for development, provide access to justice


Goal 17: Strengthen means of implementation and Sustainable Developments
